Gulab jamun(4/1/25)

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder or Khoya
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Sugar Syrup with Cardamom and Rose Water

Recipe

    * Gulab Jamun is a classic Indian sweet made by combining milk powder, flour, ghee, and milk into a soft dough. Small balls are shaped and fried slowly until golden brown, ensuring a soft, spongy center. These are then soaked in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water, allowing them to absorb sweetness. Served warm or cold, they melt in the mouth and are a festive favorite across India.

Khava Barfi

Ingredients

  • Khava (Mawa/Dried Milk)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder

Recipe

    * Khava Barfi is made with mawa (khoya), sugar, and cardamom powder cooked together until thick and aromatic. The mixture is spread onto a greased tray, cooled, and cut into neat diamond or square shapes. Often garnished with silver leaf or dry fruits, this sweet is rich, creamy, and melts in the mouth. Popular during Diwali and weddings, Barfi is one of the most loved traditional Indian sweets.

Mysore Pak

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

    * Mysore Pak is a rich South Indian sweet prepared with gram flour, ghee, and sugar syrup. The flour is roasted in hot ghee until aromatic, then carefully mixed with sugar syrup to create a smooth, dense mixture. Once set, it is cut into soft golden cubes with a crumbly, melt-in-mouth texture. Famous for its unique flavor and richness, Mysore Pak is a traditional delight served during festivals.

Recipe

Recipe

Jalebi

Recipe

Crunchy outside and juicy inside, it bursts with flavor in every bite. Badusha/Balushahi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

    * Badushah is a traditional South Indian sweet made with refined flour, yogurt, and ghee, deep-fried to perfection and coated in sugar syrup. Slightly crisp outside with a soft, flaky interior, it has a mild sweetness that makes it unique. Similar to a glazed pastry, Badushah is loved for its layered texture and is commonly prepared during festive occasions, symbolizing joy and togetherness.
